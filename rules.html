THe basics

Basketball is a 5v5 game where the team who scores the most points wins. The game starts with 2 players from each team jump for the ball.

The positions

To understand Basketball, you need to know the 5 positions.Point Guard, Shooting Guard, Small Forward, Power Forward, and Center.

  • The Point Guard is usually the smallest and fastest on the team. They always start off with ball and make the plays, they are also the best dribblers.
  • The Shooting Guard is the best shootor on their team, they are the ones that make the 3 pointers and mid range shots.
  • The Small Forward is considered the most important and versatile position, they have good ball handeling while have a decent size with good shooting.
  • The Power Forward is able to score from all levels of the court, they are very good at playing the post.
  • The Center is the biggest player on the team. They are excellent defenders and mostly rely on making layups or dunks opposed from shooting.

A combination of good players playing these positions will make a good team.
